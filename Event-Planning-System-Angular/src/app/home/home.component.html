F.A.Q

  • How do I create a new account on the platform?

    To create a new account, click on the "Register" button on the home page. Fill in the required information, and then click "Submit." You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

  • What types of events can I create using this system?

    Our platform supports a variety of event types, including weddings, conferences, parties, activities, and gatherings. When creating a new event, you can specify the type and provide detailed information such as the event name, description, location, dates, target audience, and maximum attendee count.

  • How can I manage my guest list for an event?

    After creating an event, you can manage your guest list by navigating to the event's Details page and selecting the "Invite" tab. Here, you can add or remove guests, update their information, and send invitations via email.

  • Can I update my event details after it has been created?

    Yes, you can update event details such as the event name, description, location, dates, target audience, and maximum attendee count at any time. Simply navigate to the event page and select the "Edit" option to make the necessary changes.

  • How secure is my personal information on this platform?

    We take your privacy seriously. Our platform uses industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data transmission is encrypted, and we adhere to strict privacy policies to ensure your information remains safe and confidential.
